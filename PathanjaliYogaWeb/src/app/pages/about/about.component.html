Our Journey

Promoting wellness and unity in Sankarankoil since 2010 through yoga and welfare.

The Beginning

Founded by a group of dedicated yoga practitioners and social workers, Sri Padhanjali Yoga Arakattalai started with a simple vision: to bring holistic health to every doorstep in Sankarankoil.

Over the years, we have evolved from a small yoga center into a multi-faceted trust addressing education, health awareness, and community development.
